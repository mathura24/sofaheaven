About us

In India, offering a range of high-quality furniture and home decor pieces for your living and office spaces at accessible price points. Each piece of furniture in our collection is crafted in collaboration with sought-after designers in Italy and around the world, and brings a uniquely modern design aesthetic to your home. From luxury sofas for your living room to study tables and ergonomic chairs for your home office, from home office to fabric sofa sets, Sunday offers elegant and fashionable solutions to modern home décor needs.

Each of our creations embodies a fresh design aesthetic that is both functional and contemporary. With the pandemic changing the narrative in furniture products, Sunday is offering a new interpretation of design that has emerged from a flurry of creativity, iterative thinking, and pure inspiration. In a world full of compromises, Sunday Design makes none.

we epitomize an interpretation of design that is tasteful and modern, yet accessible. Manufactured at one of the most advanced furniture factories in India, our creations express a unique blend of traditional craftsmanship techniques and modern technology. Each piece is the product of meticulously coordinated work between design and craftsmanship, created with careful attention to even the smallest nuances of modern lifestyles.

Sunday Design is more than just a brand; it is a lifestyle choice, offering bold, daring and seductive options in interior design. The clean lines, and the soulful and sleek visuals that grace every piece of furniture in our collection are distinctive of the fresh and unique perspective we bring to home décor. Our high-quality design aesthetic bestows an air of effortless elegance on each creation with no compromise on durability or affordability. Whether it be our contemporary sofas, writing desks, dressing tables, or storage drawers, you will find every piece perfect, exciting and chic additions to any interior design plan.
